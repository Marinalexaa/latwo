Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies and similar technologies are, how we use them on latwo.eu, and how you can manage your choices.

1. What are cookies and similar technologies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. We also use localStorage to store technical preferences (for example, selected language and your cookie choices).

2. What storage technologies we use

Name Type Duration Storage Purpose
latwo_cookie_consent Essential 180 days Cookie Stores your choice for cookie categories
latwo_cookie_consent_v1 Essential Until browser data is cleared localStorage Current consent record (id, version, timestamp, categories)
latwo_cookie_consent_history_v1 Essential Until browser data is cleared localStorage History of recent consent updates (up to 25 entries)
latwo_lang Functional Until browser data is cleared localStorage Stores your selected interface language
_ga Analytics 2 years Cookie (after consent) Google Analytics — tracks website visits
_ga_* Analytics 2 years Cookie (after consent) Google Analytics — stores session state
_gid Analytics 24 hours Cookie (after consent) Google Analytics — distinguishes users

3. Cookie categories by purpose

Essential cookies are required for basic site functionality, security, and storing your privacy choices.

Analytics cookies are used to analyze website traffic and improve the site (Google Analytics 4).

Marketing and media cookies are used to load third-party embedded services (for example, video) and related trackers.

4. How we collect your consent

On your first visit, you see a consent banner and can:

  • accept all cookies;
  • reject optional cookies;
  • configure categories separately.

Before consent is given, we do not activate analytics or marketing/media categories.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 for anonymized website statistics (page views, interactions, and basic technical metrics). Google Analytics is activated only after analytics consent is given.

6. Logging your choice (cookie consent audit)

To confirm consent or refusal, we may store:

  • consent identifier;
  • policy version;
  • timestamp and source of action (banner/settings);
  • language and page where the choice was made;
  • category states (essential/analytics/marketing);
  • IP hash (ip_hash) and user-agent hash (user_agent_hash).

This is used for privacy compliance and security purposes.

7. How to manage cookies

You can update your choice at any time using the cookie settings button on the website or via browser settings (view, delete, or block cookies).

Please note: disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

Instructions for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

8. Third-party services

Some pages may use third-party embedded services (for example, video). They are loaded only after relevant consent is given.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy. The latest version is always available on this page with the updated date.

10. Contact information

If you have any questions about cookie use, contact us:

  • Email: contact@latwo.eu
  • Website: latwo.eu
