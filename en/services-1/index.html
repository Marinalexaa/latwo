OUR SERVICES

AI Business &
Opportunity Analysis

The Starting Point · Team: 2-5 people · Timeline: 1-2 weeks

The first and most critical step for companies looking to implement Artificial Intelligence. We conduct a detailed breakdown of how your business operates and show you exactly where AI will deliver the highest ROI.

What's Included:

☑️ Introductory Session — Understanding Your Business

We conduct a 60-90 minute online call with executives and key employees. We study the team structure, core processes, current pain points, and company goals. We ask the right questions to see the full picture.

☑️ Process Audit — Finding the Hidden Routine

We break down the team's daily tasks in detail: what is performed manually, what is repetitive, and where the most time is wasted. We evaluate the automation potential of each process based on three parameters: labor intensity, frequency, and technical feasibility.

☑️ Opportunity Discovery — Identifying High-Impact AI Use Cases

Based on the audit, we create a comprehensive list of opportunities for AI integration: from simple team assistants to full-scale process automation. We set priorities - what should be implemented first for quick results.

☑️ Final Presentation & Documentation

We present all findings to the team. You receive a full package of documents that allows you to take action immediately - either independently or with us in the next stage.
