Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Latwo AI Consulting ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data of users of the website latwo.eu.

1. What data we collect

We may process the following categories of data:

  • data you submit in the contact form (name, email, company name, selected service, message text, choice regarding a free consultation);
  • technical data (IP address, browser type, user-agent, visited pages, date/time of request, basic device technical parameters);
  • cookie consent data (consent ID, policy version, timestamp, action source, language, page, selected categories);
  • data related to cookies and localStorage (see our Cookie Policy).

2. How we use data

We use data to process your requests, provide consultations, communicate about services, improve the website and user experience, analyze website usage (with the relevant consent), and ensure security, abuse prevention, and legal compliance.

3. Legal basis for processing

We process data based on your consent (including analytics/marketing cookies), necessity for contract performance or pre-contractual steps, our legitimate interests (security, service stability, service improvement), and legal obligations.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by our service providers that support website operations, including email delivery, hosting, analytics, and storage of technical consent logs.

5. Cookies, localStorage, and analytics

The website uses essential technical storage mechanisms (cookies/localStorage) and optional categories (analytics, marketing/media) only after your consent. Google Analytics 4 is activated only after analytics consent is granted.

6. Consent logging and security

To confirm the fact of consent/rejection, we may store technical records including ip_hash and user_agent_hash. We apply technical and organizational safeguards to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

7. Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes described in this Policy or as required by law. Retention periods for cookie/localStorage data depend on the settings described in the Cookie Policy and your browser choices.

8. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data;
  • receive your data in a portable format (where applicable) and withdraw consent at any time for processing based on consent.

9. Third-party links

The website may contain links to third-party resources. We do not control their privacy practices and recommend reviewing their policies separately.

10. Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version is always published on this page with the update date.

11. Contact information

If you have questions about this Policy or data processing, contact us:

  • Email: contact@latwo.eu
  • Website: latwo.eu
